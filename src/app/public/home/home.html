ABOUT IRIFAMS

Smart Digital Management for Irrigated Rice Farming

IRIFAMS is a web-based platform designed to modernize and simplify irrigated rice farming activities by replacing manual records and phone communication.

The system enables farmers, supervisors, administrators and stakeholders to access centralized farming information anytime.
