ABOUT IRIFAMS

Smart Digital Management for Irrigated Rice Farming

The IRIFAMS (Irrigated Rice Farm Management System) is a web-based platform developed to modernize and simplify rice farming activities. The system replaces manual records and phone communication with a fast, organized, and transparent digital solution.

Farmers, supervisors, and administrators can manage farm plots, irrigation activities, service requests, input distribution, and payment tracking in one centralized platform.
