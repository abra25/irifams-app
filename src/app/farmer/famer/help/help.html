Frequently asked questions

Click a topic to expand.

UI only
  • Complete your Profile information for accurate services.
  • Add Farms and Plots before requesting irrigation or tractor services.
  • Use Notifications to see schedules and approvals.
  • Go to Requests tab, choose service type, fill the form, then submit.
  • Status will appear after officer approval (Pending / Approved / Scheduled).
  • Check Notifications for schedule dates and updates.
  • Open Payments to view invoices and transaction history.
  • Use View receipt to open the receipt template.
  • Always keep a reference number for verification.
  • Add a Farm first, then add Plots under it.
  • Plot size helps water allocation and inputs distribution.
  • Use Map tab to view GIS markers (when integrated later).
  • Notifications show schedules, approvals and payment confirmations.
  • Use filters to search by category and status (Unread/Read).
  • Use Profile to update phone, location and membership number.
  • Use Settings for notification preferences and account controls.
  • Never share OTP or password with anyone.
